Installing

  1. 1. Buy and download

    Complete checkout. Stripe redirects you to a thanks page with your DMG download and a license key. The same email contains both.

  2. 2. Move GameKey.app to Applications

    Open the DMG and drag the icon onto the Applications folder.

  3. 3. Open GameKey and paste your license key

    The first time you launch, GameKey asks for the key from your purchase email. Paste it once; it's saved to your Keychain.

  4. 4. Install Apple's Game Porting Toolkit

    If GameKey doesn't detect GPTK, it'll show a banner with a link to Apple's developer page. GPTK is a free download from Apple.

  5. 5. Pick a launcher and click Install

    That's it. The launcher's own installer runs; sign in inside its window when it pops up.

FAQ

Does GameKey store my Steam/Epic/etc. password?

No. You sign in inside the launcher's own window, the same way you would on Windows. GameKey has no login form and no credential store.

Is the DMG signed and notarized?

Yes. The paid build is signed with our Apple Developer ID and notarized by Apple, so there's no Gatekeeper warning on first open.

I lost my license key — can I get it back?

Open the recovery page and enter the email you bought with. We'll re-send your key. If your account is in good standing the same key works on every Mac you personally own.

Why does it cost $60 when Whiskey is free?

Whiskey is great and you should consider it. GameKey charges for: a notarized one-click build, automated install recipes for all five launchers (kept up to date as the vendors change CDNs), per-launcher prefix tweaks so EA App and Ubisoft Connect don't fight you, and email support when a launcher breaks. If none of that is worth $60 to you, Whiskey is the right tool.

Will my games run?

Most DirectX 11/12 titles work through GPTK. Anti-cheat heavy games (Valorant, Fortnite chapter-restricted modes, Call of Duty multiplayer) do not — that's a launcher and anti-cheat issue, not a GameKey issue.

Is this affiliated with Whiskey or Heroic?

No. GameKey was inspired by both projects but shares no code with either. See CREDITS.md for full attributions.

Is this allowed by Steam/Epic/etc?

Running the official launcher under Wine is something each platform tolerates to varying degrees, but none ban the practice. GameKey deliberately doesn't automate logins or scrape platform APIs, which is where you get into ToS trouble.
